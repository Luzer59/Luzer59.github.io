Nemesis Perspective

Nemesis Perspective is a local multiplayer PvP VR game. The game is about bringing a new perspective in traditional boss fight setting that has been around for a couple of decades already. Now for the first time player is able to step in the boss's shoes and wreak havoc on the arena. A fast-paced and furious battle ensues.
