Nemesis Perspective

"Nemesis Perspective is an award winning local multiplayer PvP VR game. The game is about bringing a new perspective in traditional boss fight setting that has been around for a couple of decades already. Now for the first time player is able to step in the boss's shoes and wreak havoc on the arena. A fast-paced and furious battle ensues."

Now available on Steam

A long time project with almost 20 different people at different times. This project later resulted in forming of a company, Evocat Games

What I did for this project:

  • Game system design
  • Input system programming
  • Code refactoring
  • Tool programming

PRACE Mars Mania

Get your space probe safely to Mars! As the designated navigator of the Vlasiator probe, you must guide it through the hazards of space. The way is long, but the mission is important. Good luck Navigator.

  • Take command of a state of the art space probe
  • Collect data to predict and use the power of supercomputers to reveal weather-based hazards
  • After the mission is finished, send the probe on a long-term mission to outer space

Now available on Google Play

A subcontracting project for PRACE for use in different public event booths.

My part in this project:

  • Code lead
  • Gameplay programming
  • Game design
